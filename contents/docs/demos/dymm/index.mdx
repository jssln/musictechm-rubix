dymm gives drummers a new dimension of expression by enabling them to play chord progressions dynamically on electronic drum kits. Drummers can now feel more like bass guitarists!

## Maker: Clay Kardas

<img src="/images/speakers/clay-kardas.jpg" style={{height: 300 + 'px'}} />

Clay Kardas is an engineer with a B.S. in Physics from Arkansas State University and M.S. in Electrical Engineering from University of Rochester. His scientific background is almost entirely involved with signal processing of acoustic phenomena and music. He recently created Ricurrent Productions to produce technology associated with music, ergonomics, and neuroscience.
